Light table editor ?

Grid:

  • 4 × 4
  • 5 × 5
  • 6 × 6
  • 10 × 10

What is this?


Light table editor is an useful tool for creating animations on the chosen grid. Pick desired grid, paint your part of the animation and click on the save button. In text box on the right you will get the coordinates of divs which should be on in desired animation step.


You can also load animation step with pasting the coordinates into text box on the right, and clicking the load button.
